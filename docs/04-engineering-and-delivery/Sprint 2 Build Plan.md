# Sprint 2 Build Plan

## Sprint Goal

Extend CRM Intelligence from a structured data foundation into an automated and intelligent relationship management solution.

Sprint 2 introduces automation, reporting, insights and Agentforce capabilities on top of the Sprint 1 foundation.

---

# Sprint Scope

## User Stories

| ID      | Title                             | Area       |
| ------- | --------------------------------- | ---------- |
| US-2001 | Relationship Automation           | Setup      |
| US-2002 | CRM Intelligence Insights         | Reporting  |
| US-2003 | Agentforce Relationship Assistant | Agentforce |

---

# US-2001 — Relationship Automation

## Objective

Automate relationship record management to reduce manual updates and maintain accurate relationship intelligence data.

## Deliverables

- Salesforce Flow automation
- Automated relationship history creation
- Relationship health automation
- Automation testing

## Tasks

- TASK-2001 Define Automation Requirements
- TASK-2002 Build Relationship Profile Automation
- TASK-2003 Build Relationship History Automation
- TASK-2004 Validate Automation

---

# US-2002 — CRM Intelligence Insights

## Deliverables
